How to Update Your Existing Images

Replace this pattern:
background-image:url('images/name-resize20x.jpg');

With this pattern:
data-progressive="true"
data-progressive-low="images/name-placeholder.webp"
data-progressive-high="images/name.jpg"
data-progressive-webp="images/name.webp"
data-progressive-avif="images/name.avif"

Example Implementation:

<!-- Instead of current method -->
<div class="t552__blockimg" 
     style="background: url('images/img-resize20x.jpg') center center no-repeat">
</div>

<!-- Use this new method -->
<div class="t552__blockimg" 
     data-progressive="true"
     data-progressive-low="images/img-placeholder.webp"
     data-progressive-high="images/img.jpg"
     data-progressive-webp="images/img.webp"
     data-progressive-avif="images/img.avif">
</div>
